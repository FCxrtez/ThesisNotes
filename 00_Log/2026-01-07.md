# Aritmetica de bajo nivel

Invesitigar que diferentes disenios puedo usar para implementar la reduccion a un elemento del vector de 4 elementos. Gemini menciono un Multiple-Input Adder (MIA). Ademas distintas logicas para implementar redondeo, zero count, etc..

Buscar en libros:

- "Digital Arithmetic" by Miloš D. Ercegovac and Tomas Lang. 
- "Computer Arithmetic: Algorithms and Hardware Designs" by Behrooz Parhami. Seccion V toca aritmetica de reales. Seccion VII.1 High-Throughput Arithmetic. Seccion VII.4 Reconfigurable Arithmetic - Adder designs for FPGAs.

Papers:

- Leading Zero Anticipatory Logic for High-Speed Floating Point Addition" (Schmookler and Nowka). This is the seminal paper on how to predict the shift amount before the addition finishes.
- "Multi-operand Floating-Point Addition" by Fahmy and Flynn. They discuss the exact "Common Exponent" strategy we talked about, which is much faster than a tree of individual adders.
